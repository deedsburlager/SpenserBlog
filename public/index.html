About Me

Hi there! Welcome. If you are new to my blog, head on over to the Start Here page. There, you will find why this blog exists and some very important things about me!

My name is Spenser and I am so glad you are here! I am a Christian, wife, and mama living in the Bluegrass state. I have a degree in Christian Counseling, but my main work in life is a professional diaper changer, toddler chaser, and a little bit of home blogging on the side!

In my spare time I love to peruse decorating books from our local library, drink as much coffee as I can without my heart exploding, and partake in epic tickle fights with my two kids. It's my passion to help women live balanced, full and aboundant lives. On this blog you can find infromation on living a joyful and balanced life, home and style, and the occasional allergy friendly recipe!

So please, grab a mug of something yummy, stay awhile, and be encouraged!

Feel free to connect with me and introduce yourself!!
